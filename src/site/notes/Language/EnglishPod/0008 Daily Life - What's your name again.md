## 1.3 Dialogue: Forgetting Someone's Name

**A:** Nick, how's it going?  
**N:** Oh, hey. What are you doing in this neighborhood? Do you live around here?  
**A:** Actually, my office is right around the corner. It was great to meet you last week at the conference. I really enjoyed our conversation about foreign investment.  
**N:** Yeah, yeah, it was really interesting. You know, I'm in a bit of a hurry, but here's my card. We should definitely meet up again and continue our discussion.
